Celebrate Valentine's Week! ❤️

🌹 Rose Day

Rose Day marks the beginning of Valentine's Week. Lovers express their emotions by giving roses, which symbolize love, beauty, and passion.

🍫 Chocolate Day

Chocolate Day is about spreading sweetness in relationships. Couples exchange chocolates to make their bond stronger and sweeter.

🧸 Teddy Day

Teddy Day is when people give their loved ones a cute teddy bear, symbolizing warmth, comfort, and affection.

🤗 Hug Day

Hug Day is about sharing warm embraces to offer comfort, security, and love to your special one.

💋 Kiss Day

Kiss Day celebrates love and intimacy, symbolizing affection and deep emotional connection between partners.
